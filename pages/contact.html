Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5th January 2026

Briggs Creations (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how personal data is handled when you make a purchase from our website.

1. Who We Are

Briggs Creations is a UK-based business selling art prints online. For data protection purposes, Briggs Creations is the data controller for the limited personal data used to fulfil customer orders.

Contact email: contact@briggscreations.com

2. Personal Data We Collect

We do not collect or store customer personal data directly on our website.

When you make a purchase, personal and payment information (such as your name, email address, billing address, delivery address, and payment details) is collected and processed securely by Stripe, our payment provider.

We do not have access to your full payment details at any time.

3. How We Use Personal Data

We use customer personal data only to:

  • Process and fulfil orders
  • Arrange delivery of purchased art prints

To fulfil deliveries, we manually copy the necessary delivery details (such as name and address) from Stripe into our delivery provider’s system to generate a shipping label and post your order.

We do not:

  • Store customer data on our website or servers
  • Use customer data for marketing
  • Sell or share customer data for advertising or analytics

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we process personal data on the lawful basis of contractual necessity, as the information is required to fulfil your order and deliver your purchase.

5. Payments

All payments are handled securely by Stripe, which acts as an independent data controller for payment information. Stripe’s use of personal data is governed by their own privacy policy.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain customer personal data ourselves. Any personal data held by Stripe or delivery providers is retained in accordance with their own policies and legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have rights including the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing

For data held by Stripe, requests should be directed to Stripe. You may also contact us with any questions.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, please contact:

Briggs Creations Email: contact@briggscreations.com
